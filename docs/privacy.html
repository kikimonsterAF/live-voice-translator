Privacy Policy

Real Time Speech Translator ("the App") provides live voice translation.

Data We Process

  • Microphone audio is captured on-device and sent securely to cloud services to convert speech to text and translate it.
  • No accounts are required. The App does not collect names, email addresses, or persistent identifiers.

How We Use Data

  • Audio is processed transiently solely to provide speech recognition and translation.
  • We do not sell data or use it for advertising.

Third-Party Processing

The App uses Google Cloud APIs (Speech-to-Text and Translation) to perform recognition and translation. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and handled per Google’s privacy and security policies.

Data Retention

The App does not store audio or transcripts by default.

Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
